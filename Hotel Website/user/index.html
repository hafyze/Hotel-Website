Standard Room

Our standard room is comfortable and affordable. It includes a private bathroom and cable TV.

Deluxe Room

Our deluxe room is more spacious and includes additional amenities such as a mini fridge and coffee maker.

Amenities

Our dining options include a restaurant on-site, serving delicious meals and refreshing drinks throughout the day. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty breakfast, a quick lunch, or a romantic dinner, we have something to suit every taste and appetite. Our fitness center is fully equipped with state-of-the-art workout equipment, including treadmills, ellipticals, weight machines, and free weights. Whether you're looking to stay in shape during your stay or simply want to relax and unwind, our fitness center is the perfect place to do so. Our business center is equipped with all the necessary tools and technologies to help you stay productive and connected while on the road. From high-speed internet access and printing services to video conferencing and private meeting rooms, we have everything you need to conduct business with ease. Our housekeeping service is provided daily to ensure that your room is always clean and tidy, and that you have everything you need to feel at home. Whether you need fresh linens, extra towels, or any other amenities, our housekeeping team is here to help. In short, our hotel offers a wide range of amenities to make your stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. Whether you're here for business or pleasure, we have everything you need to make the most of your time with us.
